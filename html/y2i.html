Spelling should be ы (not и)

The plural ending for Masculine and Feminine nouns is usually -ы

Spell the plural ending for Masculine and Feminine nouns -ы unless the final consonant is soft or there is a spelling rule that requires spelling with the letter -и (after к, г, х, ж, ч, ш, щ)

Nominative Singular -> Nominative Plural: журнал -> журналы 'magazine', сила -> силы 'strength'

The ending for adjectives is usually -ый

Spell the Masculine Nominative Singular ending of adjectives -ый unless it is a soft stem adjective spelled with -ий or there is final stress -óй

Examples of the Masculine Nominative Singular ending of adjectives with -ый: каждый 'every', новый 'new', монументальный 'monumental'
