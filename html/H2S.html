When to spell ъ, not ь inside a word

The hard sign ъ is spelled after a consonant and before a vowel letter я, е, ё, or ю

In native Russian words, a hard sign is only found between a prefix and a root, where it shows that a consonant at the end of a prefix remains hard before a root beginning with я, е, ё, or ю

Relevant prefixes are: в-, вз-, из-, об-, от-, под-, раз-, с-

въехать 'drive in', изъятие 'withdrawal', объяснить 'explain', отъезд 'departure', подъезд 'driveway', съесть 'eat up', объём 'volume'

The hard sign ъ is spelled in some foreign borrowings

Some foreign words spelled with ъ: субъект 'subject', объект 'object', объективный 'objective'
