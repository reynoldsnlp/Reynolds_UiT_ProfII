Ending should be -ии

Special rules apply for nouns ending in -ий, -ия, -ие

The Locative Singular ending for these nouns is -ии

Nominative Singular критерий 'criterion' -> Locative Singular критерии

Nominative Singular общежитие 'dormitory' -> Locative Singular общежитии

The Dative Singular ending for these nouns is -ии if the nouns are Feminine

Nominative Singular фамилия 'last name' -> Locative and Dative Singular фамилии
