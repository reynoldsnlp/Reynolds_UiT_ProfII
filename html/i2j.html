Use of й where only и is needed

There are forms of nouns that end in -ии (not -ий)

The Locative Singular forms of Masculine nouns in -ий, Neuter nouns in -ие, and Feminine nouns in -ия all end in -ии; Feminine nouns in -ия also use the -ии ending for the Genitive/Dative/Locative Singular and Nominative/Accusative Plural

Examples of Nominative -> Locative Singular: критерий -> о критерии 'about the criterion здание -> в здании 'in the building'Россия -> в Росии 'in Russia'
