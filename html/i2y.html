Spelling should be и (not ы)

Spelling rules require that и be spelled after certain consonants

Spelling rules require that и be spelled after к, г, х (velars)

Examples of и be spelled after к, г, х: такие плохие американские книги 'such bad American books'

Spelling rules require that и be spelled after ж, ч, ш, щ (hushers)

Examples of и be spelled after ж, ч, ш, щ: широкие пляжи 'wide beaches', старшие дети похожи на отца 'the older children look like their father', чужие рабочие 'unfamiliar workers', они шили плащи 'they were sewing raincoats'

The letter ц can be followed by both и and ы, but there are rules

In the stems of words, the letter ц is usually followed by the letter и

цирк 'circus', цикл 'cycle', специальный 'special', цитата 'citation', цифра 'number, cipher', цивилизация 'civilization', циркуляция 'circulation', цилиндр 'cylinder'

A few stems contain the letter ц followed by ы: цыплёнок 'chick', цыган 'gypsy', на цыпочках 'on tiptoe'

There is a suffix -ция that is often found in borrowed words that end with -tion in English

ситуация 'situation', лекция 'lecture', информация 'information', комбинация 'combination'

In endings the letter ц is followed by ы

огурцы 'cucumbers', пальцы 'fingers', ножницы 'scissors', больницы 'hospitals'

There are certain words and endings that are spelled with и (not ы)

Nouns that end in -ь have plural forms spelled with -и

Nominative Singular -> Plural: связь -> связи 'ties', деталь -> детали 'parts, details', мысль -> мысли 'thoughts', способность -> способности 'abilities'

Feminine nouns that end in -ь have plural forms and Genitive/Dative/Locative Singular forms spelled with -и

Nominative Singular -> Genitive Singular: нефть -> нефти 'oil', жизнь -> жизни 'life'

There are some soft stem adjectives that are spelled with -ий

Some examples of soft stem adjectives are последний 'last', внутренний 'internal', ранний 'early', соседний 'neighboring', средний 'central, average', внешний 'external'

The Instrumental Plural ending always has -и at the end

Instrumental Plural examples: далёкими старнами 'far-away countries', годами 'years'

Past Tense Plural forms of verbs always end with -и

хотели 'they wanted', основали 'they established'

Neuter nouns derived from verbs end in -ие

Examples of nouns derived from verbs ending in -ие: искажение 'distortion', приближение 'approach', создание 'creation'

Pronouns are spelled with the letter и

Examples of pronouns spelled with the letter и: они 'they', о них 'about them', их 'their', с ним 'with him/it', эти 'these'

The numeral 'one' and related words are spelled with the letter и

Examples of the numeral 'one' and related words spelled with the letter и: один 'one', одни 'one (set of)/some', одинаковый 'identical'

Secondary imperfectives contain the sequence -ива- if the preceding consonant is soft or is the letter р

Examples of secondary imperfectives containing the sequence -ива-: развивать 'develop', сравнивать 'compare', рассматривать 'examine', разговаривать 'converse'

Some words contain и (not ы), and these must be memorized

Examples of common words that contain и (not ы): внутри 'inside', сила 'strength', писать 'write', развивать 'develop', разница 'difference', причина 'reason', пить 'drink', картина 'picture', красивый 'beautiful', милый 'dear', любимый 'favorite'
