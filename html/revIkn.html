Spelling of the letter е is incorrect in an unstressed syllable

The letter и should be spelled (not е)

All Present tense forms of second conjugation verbs (except First Person Singular and Third Person Plural) are spelled with the letter и

Examples of Third Person Singular forms of second conjugation verbs spelled with the letter и: видит 's/he sees', смотрит 's/he looks', выглядит 's/he/it looks like', значит 'it means'

There is a prefix that is spelled при- (not to be confused with another prefix that is spelled пре-)

Example of the use of the prefix при-: принадлежать 'belong to'

The spelling of и and foreign borrowings tends to follow the spelling of i in English

Examples of the spelling of и in foreign borrowings: пингвин 'penguin', интеллигентный 'intelligent'

There are stems that are spelled with the letter и

Examples of stems spelled with the letter и: большинство 'majority', умирать 'die', удивительный 'surprising', увеличивать 'increase'
