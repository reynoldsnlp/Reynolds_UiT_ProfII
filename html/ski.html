There are adverbs that end in -ски (not -ский)

There are some adverbs that sound like similar adjectives, but end in -ски (not -ский)

Adverbs with the pattern по-Xски meaning 'do something in an X way' are most often used to describe proficiency in a language

Examples of adverbs with the pattern по-Xски (be sure to remember the hyphen after по-): по-русски 'in Russian', по-английски 'in English', по-человечески 'in a human way'

There are also some adverbs that just end in -ски (without по-)

Examples of adverbs in -ски: фантастически 'fantastic', дьявольски 'devlishly', всячески 'in every possible way'
