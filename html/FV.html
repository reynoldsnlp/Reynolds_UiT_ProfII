Fleeting vowel should be deleted

A fleeting vowel is a vowel that is present in some forms but absent in others, as in: немец, немца 'German (Nominative Singular, Genitive Singular)' and ручка, ручек 'pen (Nominative Singular, Genitive Plural)'

When a syllable containing а fleeting vowel of o or e (sometimes и) is followed by an ending containing a vowel, the fleeting vowel may be dropped

Nouns

Nouns that contain a fleeting vowel and end in a consonant in the Nominative Singular delete the fleeting vowel in all other forms

Nominative Singular -> Genitive singular отец -> отца 'father', день -> дня 'day', сон -> сна 'sleep', лёд -> льда 'ice', платок -> платка 'scarf', церковь -> церкви 'church'

Nouns ending in a consonant in the Genitive Plural delete the fleeting vowel in all other forms (unless the Genitive Plural ends in a husher followed by -ей)

Genitive Plural -> Nominative Singular девушек -> девушка 'girl', коробок -> коробка 'box', семей -> семья 'family', окон -> окно 'window'

Adjectives

Short form adjectives with a fleeting vowel in the Masculine Singular delete the fleeting vowel in all other forms

Masculine Singular -> Feminine Singular доволен -> довольна 'satisfied', смешон -> смешна 'funny

The fleeting vowel и in the Nominative Masculine Singular form of adjectives ending in -ий is dropped in all other forms

Nominative Masculine Singular -> Nominative Feminine Singular собачий -> собачья 'dog's'

Other

Fleeting vowels occur in the pronouns весь -> вся 'all' and чей -> чья 'whose'

Fleeting vowels occur in the numerals один -> одна 'one', восемь -> восьми 'eight', сот -> сто 'hundred'

The fleeting vowel и in Genitive Plural яиц 'eggs' is dropped in all other forms, such as Nominative Singular яйцо

The fleeting vowel и in Masculine Singular достоин 'worthy' is dropped in all other forms, such as Feminine достойна

Fleeting vowels can also appear in prepositions and in the prefixes and roots of verbs, as in со студентом 'with the student', подобрать 'pick up', подберу 'I will pick up'
