A single letter (not a double letter) should be spelled

The short forms of Past Passive Participles are spelled with a single letter н

The short forms of Past Passive Participles are spelled with -н, -но, -на, -ны (Masculine, Neuter, Feminine, Plural)

Examples of the short forms of Past Passive Participles are spelled with a single letter н: написано 'written', запрещено 'forbidden', созданы 'created'

Some words contain a double letter, but only in one place

The spelling of some words is confusing because there is a double letter in one place but a single letter in another place

Examples of confusing words with double and single letters: искусство 'art', иностранный 'foreign'

Some words do not contain double letters

The spelling of a single letter should be memorized for some words

Examples of words that do not contain a double letter: количество 'quantity', проблема 'problem', раса 'race', рекомендовать 'recommend', эмиграция 'emigration'
