Palatalization of a consonant should be preserved

If a consonant is palatalized (soft), this must be shown by using the correct vowel letters or the soft sign ь

Paired consonants followed by the letters -я, -е, -ё, -и, -ю, -ь are palatalized

The л in земля 'land' is palatalized, as we see in forms like Dative/Locative Singular земле, Instrumental Singular землёй, Genitive Singular земли, Accusative Singular землю, Genitive Plural земель

Paired consonants are the consonants that can be either palatalized or not palatalized: б, в, д, з, л, м, н, п, р, с, т, ф

Note these correspondences between “hard” vowel letters that indicate that the preceding paired consonant is hard, as opposed to “soft” vowel letters that indicate that the preceding paired consonant is palatalized (soft)
