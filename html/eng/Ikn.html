Spelling of the letter и is incorrect in an unstressed syllable

The letter e should be spelled (not и)

There are stems that are spelled with the letter e

Examples of stems spelled with the letter e: насeления 'population', впeчатление 'impression', детей 'children' (Genitive Plural), словосочeтание 'collocation', повесeлиться 'have fun', подвергаться 'undergo'

There are some verbs that end in -еть (not -ить)

Examples of verbs that end in -еть: зависеть 'depend on', видеть 'see'

There is a prefix that is spelled пре- (not to be confused with another prefix that is spelled при-)

Examples of the use of the prefix пре-: превращаться 'turn into', преследовать 'persecute'

There are suffixes that are spelled -ческий and -чество

Examples of the suffixes that are spelled -ческий and -чество: лирический 'lyrical', человечествo 'humankind'

The spelling of е and и in foreign borrowings tends to follow the spelling of e and i in English

Examples of the spelling of е and и in foreign borrowings: полемический 'polemical', интерпретировать 'interpret'

The letter a should be spelled (not и)

After the letter ч in some words, the letter a should be spelled (not и)

Examples of the spelling of a (not и) after the letter ч: началось 'it began', начали 'they started' (note that the a is stressed in the word начáло 'beginning')
