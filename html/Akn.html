Some spelling errors arise because the vowel letters a and o sound the same in unstressed syllables

An unstressed o is pronounced like a, but spelled with o (not with a)

Some roots, prefixes, and suffixes will have stress on ó in another word or form, showing that the vowel is actually o

The word водá 'water' is spelled with an o in the first syllable, and we know that the vowel is an o because it is pronounced as ó when it is stressed in related words вóдка 'vodka' and вóдный 'water (adj.)'

The prefix под- is spelled with an o in подписáть 'sign', and we know that the vowel is an o because it is pronounced as ó when it is stressed in пóдпись 'signature'

The Instrumental Singular ending -ом is spelled with an o in слóвом 'word', and we know that the vowel is an o because it is pronounced as ó when it is stressed in the Instrumental Singular окнóм 'window'

For some words, it is necessary to memorize that the vowel is spelled o

The spelling of unstressed o in the first syllable must be memorized for these words: один 'one', который 'which, who', когда 'when', количество 'quantity'

An unstressed a is spelled with a (not with o)

Some roots will have stress on á in another word or form, showing that the vowel is actually a

We know that the word правительство 'government' is spelled with an a because of the related word прáвить 'govern'

We know that the word означать 'indicate' is spelled with an a because of the related word знáчит 'it means'

We know that the word составлять 'comprise' is spelled with an a because of the related word стáвить 'place, make stand'

For some words, it is necessary to memorize that the vowel is spelled a

The spelling of unstressed a must be memorized for these words: предлагать 'suggest', предполагать 'suppose'

Some grammatical endings contain the letter o; others contain the letter a

These grammatical endings contain the letter о: Neuter Nominative and Accusative Singular -oe -o, Masculine and Neuter Instrumental Singular and Locative Singular -ом, Feminine Genitive, Dative, Locative, and Instrumental Singular -ой

новое окно 'new window', окном 'window', о новом окне 'about the new window', о новой книге 'about the new book'

These grammatical endings contain the letter a: Feminine Nominative Singular -aя -а, Dative Plural -ам, Instrumental Plural -ами, Locative Plural -ах

старая книга 'old book', книгам 'books', книгами 'books', книгах 'books'

Borrowed words usually follow the spelling of the language of origin

Use the same spelling of vowels o and a as in English in borrowed words

пропаганда 'propaganda', практический 'practical', романтический 'romantic'
