A double letter (geminate) should be spelled

There are certain places where double letters are spelled in Russian

When a prefix ends with the same or similar sound as that at the beginning of a root or there is a compound word, it may be necessary to spell a double letter

Examples of prefix-root combinations and compounds where double letters are spelled: рассказывать 'narrate', исследовать 'research', восстание 'uprising', поддерживать 'support', здравоохранение 'healthcare'

Double letters are spelled in certain words that must be memorized

Examples of words spelled with double letters: искусство 'art', режиссёр 'director', Россия 'Russia'

Double нн is spelled in certain adverbs

Examples of double нн spelled in adverbs: постоянно 'constantly', именно 'precisely', особенно 'especially'

Double нн is spelled in some adjectives

Examples of double нн spelled in adjectives: современный 'contemporary', нетрадиционный 'non-traditional', государственный 'belonging to the state', квалифицированный 'qualified'

Double нн is spelled in the long forms of Past Passive Participles

Examples of double нн spelled in the long forms of Past Passive Participles: связанный 'bound', определённый 'determined'

The spelling of double letters in borrowed words often follows their spelling in English

Examples of spelling of double letters in borrowed words: иммигранты 'immigrants', интеллектуальный 'intellectual', интеллигентный 'intelligent', комментарий 'comment', территория 'territory', профессиональный 'professional', некорректный 'not correct'
