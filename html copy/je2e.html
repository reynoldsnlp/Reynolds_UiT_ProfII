Spelling should be with the letter e (not э)

In native Russian words, spelling of e is most common

Except for words related to этот 'this, that' and foreign borrowings beginning with э-, we almost always spell the letter e

Examples of words spelled with the letter e (not э): проeкт 'project', должeн 'should', дебют 'debut', ерунда 'nonsense', сюжет 'subject', главные 'main' (Plural), по-моему 'in my opinion'
