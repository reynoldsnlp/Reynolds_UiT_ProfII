When to spell the letter a (not o) in unstressed syllables

Some spelling errors arise because the vowel letters a and o sound the same in unstressed syllables

Some roots will have stress on á in another word or form, showing that the vowel is actually a

We know that the word правительство 'government' is spelled with the letter a because of the related word прáвить 'govern'

We know that the word означать 'indicate' is spelled with the letter a because of the related word знáчит 'it means'

We know that the word составлять 'comprise' is spelled with the letter a because of the related word стáвить 'place, make stand'

For some words, it is necessary to memorize that the vowel is spelled with the letter a

The spelling of unstressed a with the letter a must be memorized for these words: предлагать 'suggest', предполагать 'suppose'

These grammatical endings contain the letter a: Feminine Nominative Singular -aя -а, Dative Plural -ам, Instrumental Plural -ами, Locative Plural -ах

старая книга 'old book', книгам 'books', книгами 'books', книгах 'books'

Borrowed words usually follow the spelling of the language of origin

Use the same spelling of vowels o and a as in English in borrowed words

пропаганда 'propaganda', практический 'practical', романтический 'romantic'
